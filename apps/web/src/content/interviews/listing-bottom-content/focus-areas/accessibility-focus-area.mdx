## Accessibility Interview Questions for Front End Interviews

Mastering accessibility is essential for front end developers, and it's a topic that frequently comes up in interviews. Our Accessibility focus area is designed to help you prepare effectively by offering targeted practice on key topics like semantic HTML, ARIA, and screen reader compatibility.

You'll have the opportunity to code directly in-browser, working through carefully selected questions that reflect real interview scenarios. Each question is paired with detailed, curated solutions provided by ex-interviewers from top tech companies, ensuring you gain a deep understanding of best practices in accessibility.

Whether you're new to accessibility or looking to refine your skills, this focus area provides the comprehensive preparation you need to excel in your front end interview.

### Key Features

**1. Quality materials by credible engineers:** All the materials on the platform—including every question, solution, test case and question lists—have been written and curated by big tech ex-interviewers who have conducted numerous interviews at top tech companies. We strive in every piece to deliver streamlined, high quality content.

**2. Diverse and relevant practice questions:** Our focus area offers a wide array of realistic, high-impact questions that align with the latest trends in front end interviews. These carefully selected questions ensure you're thoroughly prepared for all aspects of accessibility.

**3. Detailed and insightful solutions:** Each question in this focus area is accompanied by a comprehensive, well-explained solution. These solutions not only provide the correct answers but also explore the reasoning and strategies behind them, helping you develop a deeper understanding of the subject.

**4. Realistic interview simulation:** Our in-browser coding workspace provides a practical environment that closely replicates actual interview scenarios. This setup allows you to become familiar with the tools and situations you'll encounter in real accessibility interviews.

**5. Immediate feedback with automated testing:** To enhance your learning experience, we offer one-click automated test cases that allow you to quickly evaluate your solutions. This instant feedback helps you identify and correct any errors, ensuring continuous progress in your preparation.
