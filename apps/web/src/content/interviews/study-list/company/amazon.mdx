### Known questions

Amazon's Online Assessment round is conducted on HackerRank and is **always the same two questions**: (1) form validation and (2) accordion, and you should know how to build them in Vanilla JavaScript. Practice GreatFrontEnd's [Contact Form](/questions/user-interface/contact-form), [Signup Form](/questions/user-interface/signup-form) and [Accordion](/questions/user-interface/accordion) user interface questions.
